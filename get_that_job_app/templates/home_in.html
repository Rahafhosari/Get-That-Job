About Get That Job

Fresh graduates face many obstacles after graduation. one of the most critical struggles is the gap between the academic background and the skills needed for interviews,... Our team in Get That Job addressed the problem and worked hard to get the best solution. Out of our duty as a part of this community we took the responsibility to train youth and develop their needed skills and enable them to Get That Job! Our goal is to create a platform that seeks to fill the gap between academic knowledge and interview skills, by creating valuable content to develop the needed skills. Content is displayed as videos, articles and sketches. This platform offers online consulting by putting users in virtual interviews with professionals to evaluate the users and give them feedback on their strengths and weaknesses.
